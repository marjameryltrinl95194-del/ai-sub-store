FAQ

How and when do I get paid? +

Commissions are paid in USDT (TRC20) to your provided wallet address. Payouts are processed weekly on Mondays for all confirmed sales from the previous week. Minimum payout: $10.

What counts as a valid referral? +

A referral is counted when someone clicks your unique link and makes a purchase within 30 days. The 30-day cookie is stored in their browser. If they clear their cookies, the referral may not be tracked.

What products can I promote? +

All products on AISubHub: ChatGPT Plus/Pro, Claude Pro/Max, and Gemini Pro. Commission is 10% regardless of which product the referral purchases.

Can I promote on any platform? +

Yes! Social media, YouTube, blogs, Telegram, Discord, Twitter/X, Reddit — anywhere your audience is. Just avoid spam and follow each platform's guidelines.

How do I track my referrals? +

Visit your Affiliate Dashboard to see real-time stats: clicks, conversions, and estimated earnings.
