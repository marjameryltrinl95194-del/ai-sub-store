## 🚀 What is AISubHub?

AISubHub lets you buy **ChatGPT Plus/Pro**, **Claude Pro**, and **Gemini Pro** subscriptions at **up to 40% off** retail prices — paid with **cryptocurrency (USDT)**.

✅ **Personal account** — not a shared/mirror account  
✅ **No credentials needed** — your password stays yours  
✅ **Crypto payment** — USDT (TRC20 / BEP20 / Polygon)  
✅ **Full refund** — if recharge fails, you get your money back  
✅ **8 languages** — English, 中文, Русский, Deutsch, Español, Français, العربية, 日本語
